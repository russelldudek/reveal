Where will value be lost?

A one-page review for converting AI enthusiasm into a decision-quality portfolio record.

1 · Work and outcome

What decision, workflow, delay, quality failure, or recurring exception changes? Who owns the outcome? What is the baseline?

2 · Value hypothesis

What business consequence improves? What labor, cycle time, quality, risk, revenue, service, or learning measure decides whether the work matters?

3 · AI role and fit

Should AI retrieve, generate, classify, predict, optimize, recommend, or act? Why AI rather than deterministic automation or process repair?

4 · Human authority

Who approves, reviews, overrides, escalates, stops, accepts risk, or owns an exception? What consequence of error is unacceptable?

5 · Architecture and data

Which data, identity, integration, cloud, model/provider, API, observability, cost, support, and rollback boundaries apply?

6 · Governance and evidence

What security, privacy, legal, regulatory, responsible-AI, evaluation, and acceptance evidence is required before release?

7 · Delivery and ownership

What is the smallest scalable increment? Build or buy? Who owns product, platform, operations, incidents, vendors, and lifecycle decisions?

8 · Adoption and change

What roles, standard work, training, champions, communication, reinforcement, exceptions, and workflow friction must change?

9 · Yield measures

Expected vs realized value; intake-to-decision; proof-to-production; retained use; task success; override behavior; quality; cost; rework; learning velocity.

10 · Next decision

What evidence is missing? Who supplies it? What condition must be met? What is the review date? What will make the initiative stop?
