## Typography evidence and decision
The public site presents a modern, geometric, high-weight sans-serif system with generous headline scale and compact utility text. The campaign uses the locally available, open-source Lato family as a documented structural substitute. No proprietary or extracted font files are committed.

## Photography / illustration character
Revel IT uses dark technology imagery, particle-wave fields, fiber-optic light paths, translucent geometric overlays, indigo/purple depth, and coral directional accents. The campaign uses the official hero imagery as a low-opacity recognition layer and translates the same visual grammar into an original Enterprise AI Yield System.

## Geometry and motion
Official assets use rounded rectangular frames, segmented geometric overlays, roads/paths, waves, and directional coral markers. Campaign motion uses a scanning line and a deforming retained-value field tied directly to portfolio scenarios; it does not animate the company mark.

## Independent-candidate distinction
Every surface identifies the work as an independent candidate vision for a confidential client opportunity represented by Revel IT. It does not imply Revel IT or end-client authorship, sponsorship, or endorsement.
