Laura,

The Director of AI Solutions & Delivery mandate is larger than selecting platforms or accelerating pilots. The organization needs a decision and delivery system that turns executive demand into a governed portfolio—one that makes value, architecture, human authority, adoption, operating ownership, and evidence visible before production pressure exposes the gaps.

At Compunetics, I led engineering, manufacturing, quality, customer delivery, and complex technical programs in advanced electronics and high-reliability environments. At Amazon, I led launch and execution in a 24/7 customer-backward operation supporting approximately five million second-day orders annually, building the visibility, cadence, escalation, and standard work required at scale. Today at Vape-Jet, I connect production, purchasing, quality, customer success, support, engineering issue flow, ERP/Odoo, CRM, collaboration systems, telemetry, and AI-assisted workflows into a more coherent operating system.

Through DudeWorth, I have also developed AI-readiness and opportunity frameworks across value, feasibility, data readiness, governance, adoption burden, reuse, and time-to-value, along with agentic workflow patterns using retrieval, human judgment, evaluation, escalation, and governance rails. The emphasis is practical: attach AI to accountable work and decisions, then measure whether the intervention survives real operating conditions.

I have not held this exact title inside a Fortune 500 global manufacturer, and I would not present adjacent experience as equivalent enterprise ownership. My case is transferability: more than two decades making complex technical work legible across engineering, operations, quality, customers, vendors, and leadership—combined with current hands-on AI operating transformation. I know how to learn a system without flattening it, preserve expert authority, and build mechanisms that turn ambition into repeatable execution.

My independent candidate vision presents an Enterprise AI Yield System: a role-specific model showing where value is retained or lost across qualification, governance, delivery, workflow embedding, and realization. The accompanying 120-day plan and AI Yield Review make my reasoning inspectable without pretending to know the confidential client’s undisclosed processes. The materials are available through my portfolio at russelldudek.github.io.

I would welcome the opportunity to pressure-test this thesis with you and the client’s executive team.
